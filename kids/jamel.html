Jamel Leslie

Where Have I Come From?, 'Modern Warfare'

"My sculpture is about pain and hardship, it represents my struggle of growing up as a young black male, on a disadvantaged estate in South London where I felt socially isolated from the rest of the world and had to go against authorities in order to make ends meet. Everyday was a battle against everyone else and against my own guilt."

Where Am I Going?, 'Warrior Phoenix'

"Through my experience growing up, I've seen a lot of dark things which made me die inside as a person, so I had to use the power and courage I used to fight authority, to face new challenges and go against the trend on my estate instead. It takes courage to adapt to anything that is thrown my way."

My 'Walk A Mile In My Shoes' Experience

"This project has been a great influence and has helped me develop my creative side and has given me the opportunity to explore different ideas and bring them to life. I have had so much fun working with Taz and the rest of the group. It has been inspirational, and this project has opened my eyes and has unlocked a secret talent I didn't even know I had. I am an artist too."
