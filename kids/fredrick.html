Fredrick Turay

Where Have I Come From?, 'In Cold Blood'

"This is my past. I lived a cold life and I saw a lot of blood. People stabbed you in the back."

Where Am I Going?, 'Angel of Love'

"This sculpture shows how I am changing my life around. I come to The Urban Academy every day to try and do better. I am moving into a new world that will be like heaven. It is also about me being a new person from head to toe. It is about me moving to a new place where there is love, not hate and I am filled with that love, and the hate goes."

My 'Walk A Mile In My Shoes' Experience

"This experience has been great. I never thought I would have been able to put my emotions on paper and then a pair of trainers. It helped me to remember why I am changing my life. Taz is safe."
