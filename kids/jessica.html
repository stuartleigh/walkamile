Jessica Richards

Where Have I Come From?, 'Bondage Beauty'

"This sculpture was inspired by my past and my trials. At the age of 9, I became the carer for my mother who has schizophrenia. I felt very confused and trapped but thought I just had to manage. I would put on a mask with make-up and a smile to pretend I was grown up and could take care of us both"

Where Am I Going?, 'Break-Through'

"In my future I see hope and riches, I see love and prosperity. I am royalty and I will be treated like a queen. I am going to be the best woman I can be and I will hold my head high and look forward. I am beautiful and shining."

My 'Walk A Mile In My Shoes' Experience

"Making these sculptures has allowed me to do something I thought I wasn't capable of doing. It has allowed me to see who I am and who I strive to become."
