Anael Wuta-Ofei

Where Have I Come From?, 'The Sunken Life'

"My sculpture tells a story about a boy's journey through struggles, weights of the world plus the emotional ups and downs of life."

Where Am I Going?, 'The True Life Of Success'

"In my past trainers, about my struggles, was the beginning of a journey through a tunnel of doubts. But everyone knows there's always a light at the end of every tunnel. My light consists of a stress and weight free life which is why I choose the tropical life to represent my design."

My 'Walk A Mile In My Shoes' Experience

"The whole experience of meeting someone from the Loewy branding company, learning to create messages, statements and telling stories through trainers plus creating new expressions through designs was a good way to think outside the box of normal footwear. I would recommend this project to other students from communities." "I learnt that anything can be art. I learnt a new way to express myself. I really recommend this project."
