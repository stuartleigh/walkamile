Fungai Benhura

Where Have I Come From?, 'African Spirit'

"I decided to do the shoe sculpture this way because it tells who I am and where I come from in Zimbabwe."

Where Am I Going?, 'Shona'

"This sculpture shows how my brain works in an abstract way but is still African and part of my tribe."

My 'Walk A Mile In My Shoes' Experience

"It was a good experience. Working with Taz was great and it helped me discover other creative ways of doing things. I learnt a lot of things from her that will help me. It helped me to express where I have come from and where I am going to go in my life and that I don't have to give up my past but use it in my future. This project means you can express things you don't know how and can't say."
