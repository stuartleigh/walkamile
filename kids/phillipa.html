Phillipa Moore

Where Have I Come From?, 'The Kiss of Death'

"At this time in my life I was not happy and had nobody to love me. I was alone and didn't know why my mum went away and never called. My sister didn't want to talk to me and I didn't want to be around people. I locked myself away from the world. I was dying like the flowers, and the bad was crawling on me like the bugs on my sculpture."

Where Am I Going?, 'Touched by the Spirit – Resurrection'

"I found someone to talk to and help me with getting to know myself and that was Annie, my best friend. She was always there when I needed someone to talk to. She gave me somewhere to live when I had nowhere to go. She gave me back life and so the flowers came to life again. When someone gives you their time it is like a cocoon where you can feel safe to become a butterfly."

My 'Walk A Mile In My Shoes' Experience

"Taz and Vickie were great. They always made me feel welcome at class. When I was upset they made me feel better and I felt like I could always talk to them even though I never said much they would just know and would make it better. Taz made me see things a different way and that calmed me down a lot. Thank you for letting me do the workshops."
